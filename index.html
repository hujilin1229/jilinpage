Jilin Hu is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science at Aalborg University. Prior to that, he was a Research Assocaite at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (UAE) under the supervision of Prof. Jianbing Shen. He obtained the Ph.D. degree from Aalborg Unviersity in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Christian S. Jensen and Prof. Bin Yang. From Oct. 2017 to April. 2018, he visited University of California, Berkeley under the supervision of Prof. Alexandre Bayen.

Research interest: Spatio-Temporal Data Management, Traffic Data Analysis, Graph Neural Network
